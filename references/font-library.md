# Curated Font Library

A curated collection of ~80 distinctive free/open-source fonts from 10+ foundries. This is a starting point, not a constraint -- real fonts outside this list are acceptable. Fonts banned in `anti-patterns.md` are never acceptable.

## Persona Fit

- **Bierut (The Servant):** Fonts that disappear -- clean, restrained, good at scale. Workhorses that serve the content without competing with it.
- **Scher (The Expressionist):** Fonts that ARE the design -- display-first, bold, maximum character. Typography as the primary visual element.
- **Collins (The Futurist):** Fonts that signal the future -- contemporary, often variable, digitally native. Forward-looking without being gimmicky.
