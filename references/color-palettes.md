# Color Palette Library

A curated collection of ~35 distinctive palettes organized by emotional territory. This is a starting point, not a constraint. Custom palettes driven by the brand thesis are encouraged. Cliche industry palettes from `anti-patterns.md` are never acceptable.

## Persona Fit

- **Bierut (The Servant):** Restrained palettes. Fewer colors doing more work. High contrast ratios. Let one color carry the load while the rest support.
- **Scher (The Expressionist):** Collision palettes. Unexpected color relationships. Bold primary choices that create tension and energy.
- **Collins (The Futurist):** Contemporary palettes. Unusual accent choices. Strong dark theme translations. Colors that feel native to digital environments.
