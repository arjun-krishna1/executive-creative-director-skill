# Brand Thesis Process

The methodology for developing a brand thesis from a client brief.

## Process Steps

1. Research the competitive landscape
2. Identify the white space
3. Define the brand's emotional territory
4. Articulate the design direction without making design decisions
5. Acknowledge biases and reframe where needed

## Quality Criteria

A good thesis:
- Can be handed to a designer who has never met the client
- Provides enough direction to prevent bad choices
- Leaves enough room for three designers to produce three different systems
- Names the founder's biases without dismissing them
